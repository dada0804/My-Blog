About Me

Learner | Runner | WRiter

I love learning.

Learning for me is something painful yet it brings me the deepest joy. Indeed, the more I suffer, the more I love!

I love learning Ukulele, practicing the simplest rhythme for several hours until it becomes natural.

I love learning something new by reading books. However, the more I read, the more I am getting curious : )

I love learning new places by travelling, hiking, climbing mountains, living in hostel, having dinners in the convenient store etc. In a word, I don't fancy comfortable trips :P

I love running.

I started running in 2014 but just stopped regular run in the second half of 2015 due to an overwhelming job. However, from this year on, I have kept my monthly goal of 200KM for 6 months!

I am kind of disappointed that despite running so much, I barely lose weight T_T However, it won't bother me any more. All I enjoy is the feeling of sweating and running to embrace the wind.

Here you can see my running on Strava.

I love writing.

I am probably the only reader of my articles. I don't even want to call them articles because it is just mumbo-jumbo for others.

For me, I just need to clean my thoughts by pouring them into words so that I have more space to think : ) I usually write a lot when I need to do self-reflection, organize my learning notes, sorting out my thoughts on books and movies.

I will try my best to leave something useful here!
